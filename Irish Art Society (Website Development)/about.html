About Us :

This is a forum for the members of Irish Art Society & those who have a high interest in art to post paintings and dicuss items of interest with other members of the Society. Irish Art Society (IAS) has been in existence for over 50 years. Membership is by invitation only following submission of a portfolio of recent work and is limited to 70 members. The Society hosts an annual exhibition of members' work in addition to tutorials, workshops and demonstrations for its members. Members works are widely represented and have been exhibited in the RHA, Oireachtas and The Watercolour Society of Ireland exhibitions. Enquiries to IAsociety@gmail.com Please send us some of your images or events that may be of interest to our members and we would be delighted to post them on our Page. Guidelines for submissions: * Maximum three paintings a day * Include dimensions and media * Do not post entire albums

The role of Culture Ireland :

Culture Ireland is the division of the Department responsible for the promotion of the arts and artists overseas. Culture Ireland creates and supports opportunities for Irish artists and companies to present their work at strategic international showcases, festivals, venues and arts markets and support artists right across the creative spectrum including visual art, theatre, literature, music, dance, opera and film.

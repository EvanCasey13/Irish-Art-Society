The Irish Art Society is an important community in visual arts in Ireland which seeks to preserve and appreciate Irish Art and Art from different periods in Western Art History.

Us at The Irish Art Society are always looking for new members with an enriched passion & appreciation for Irish Art and Art from other periods of History and as a result we have dropped the price of our membership to half at just thirty euro for three months membership. You can find our contact information in the About Our Organisation tab located above where you can get in touch with us to setup your membership with us. We hope to hear from you all very soon.
- President William Ashford

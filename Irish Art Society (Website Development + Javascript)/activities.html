Below are a list of the Activities that take place at our Organisation on a day-to-day and biweekly basis

Activities :

  • Lecture & Study of Various Paintings held every Monday at 5:30pm

  • Open library containing various books with excellent information on many eras of Painting for members

  • Every Wednesday the art society becomes a place for members to hone their skills, develop their techniques and collaborate with other Artists

  • Annual Arts Festival held twice a year at Christmas and Summer

  • Every Saturday a meeting is held at 7:00pm to discuss any ideas & problems that members or staff may have

  • Our art club meets at least once a month, with more meetings if a project requires it.

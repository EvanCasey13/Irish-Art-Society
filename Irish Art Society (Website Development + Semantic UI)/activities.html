Below are a list of the Activities that take place at our Organisation on a day-to-day and biweekly basis

Activities :

  • Lecture & Study of Various Paintings held every Monday at 5:30pm

  • Open library containing various books with excellent information on many eras of Painting for members

  • Every Wednesday the art society becomes a place for members to hone their skills, develop their techniques and collaborate with other Artists

  • Annual Arts Festival held twice a year at Christmas and Summer

  • Every Saturday a meeting is held at 7:00pm to discuss any ideas & problems that members or staff may have

  • Our art club meets at least once a month, with more meetings if a project requires it.


Why you should become involved in Our Activities :

  1. Art influences society by changing opinions, instilling values and translating experiences across space and time. Research has shown art affects the fundamental sense of self. Painting, sculpture, music, literature and the other arts are often considered to be the repository of a society's collective memory.

  2. The arts bring us joy, help us express our values, and build bridges between cultures. The arts are also a fundamental component of a healthy community—strengthening them socially, educationally, and economically—benefits that persist even in difficult social and economic times.

  3. Art expresses the non-verbal. And despite our heavy emphasis on the verbal and written, there’s a vast realm of human experience that can only be understood and supported without words.
